SeeNot helps you keep your phone use aligned with your own intent.

Tell SeeNot what you want to do and what you want to avoid. During the session,
it analyzes your screen in context and intervenes when you drift away from your
goal.

Key features:
- Voice-first intent input (text input also supported)
- AI screen understanding based on actual page content
- Graduated intervention levels: reminder, auto-back, return home
- Flexible limits: session cap, content cap, and daily limits
- False-positive feedback loop to improve future judgments
- Local-first data storage on device

Permissions:
- Accessibility Service: detect foreground app and screen state
- Display over other apps: show floating button and intervention overlays

An OpenAI-compatible vision model API key is required.
